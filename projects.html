PROJECTS

Here are some of my more noteworthy projects from the past few years.


Procedural Fighting Game AI

A proof-of-concept AI with "habits" that mimics how fighting games are played by humans.



Slide Sanctuary

A curious browser game about moving around the rooms of a dungeon like a slide puzzle.



Fighting games, depth vs. complexity, and how the conflation of the two leads to anti-accessibility rhetoric

An article I put together about the ways in which fighting games continue to exclude players through their control schemes, discussing how players confuse "depth" and "complexity".



GrannyQuest

An Alternate Reality Game (ARG) built for a community of players who unravel the mystery of a mysterious . I directed the gameplay team and was in charge of coordinating puzzle design and their rollout onto the internet, as well as communicating with the art and coding teams.




Airships & Appetites

The framework for a Dungeons & Dragons campaign with a cute twist, designed for my Storytelling in Games class.


Mapping Environmental Injustice

A term project in conjunction with the Chelsea Department of Housing & Community Development. Using environmental and demographic data, our team created maps to help identify vulnerable communities.



Collective Wisdom

A cozy browser game about building societies using knowledge.



Rainbow Ripple

A playful, interactive toy that lets players create satisfying ripples.



Growforth

A choose-your-own adventure game made in Twine where players meet curious characters across a dreamlike world. It's quite rough, but it was my first time directing a development team and I think we pulled it together.
