ABOUT

What matters to me:

I've always been fascinated by the ability of interactive technology and media and its impact on our lives. Whether it's an app to help someone through their day or a powerful story told through a game, interactive tech shapes our world. I take particular interest in the intricacies of human-computer interaction and games as a rhetorical art form! Our creations have the chance to not only be functional user experiences, but emotional user experiences.

I've long had an interest in both computer science and game development, but my time at WPI has allowed me to simulataneously pursue both majors in greater depth. I hope to one day create a program or game remembered for how it improved the lives of others!
I aspire to inspire in everything I do!
